Why Ellidun Microlots

The difference is in the details

🤝

Direct Trade

We pay premium prices directly to smallholder farmers - no middlemen. Farmers earn 30–60% above commodity pricing.

📍

Full Traceability

Every bag ships with a farm dossier: GPS coordinates, processing method, harvest date, and cupping scores.

☕

Specialty Grade Only

We source exclusively 84+ SCA-scored lots. Our Q-Graders cup every incoming lot before it's listed.

How It Works

From farm gate to your roastery

01

Farm Visit

Our sourcing team travels to origin each harvest season cupping at the farm gate.

02

Lot Selection

Only the finest micro and nano lots are selected — often just one or two bags per farmer.

03

Quality Control

Independent Q-Grade assessment in origin country and upon arrival.

04

Export & Delivery

Green coffee ships via climate-controlled sea freight. Spot lots available ex-warehouse.
