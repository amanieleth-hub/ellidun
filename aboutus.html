Our Story

From origin soil to global specialty cups

Ellidun Microlot Coffee Export began with a simple idea: Ethiopian farmers producing extraordinary coffee deserved a direct path to specialty roasters worldwide.

We saw a broken chain — too many middlemen, too little transparency, and farmers not receiving true value for their craft. Ellidun was built to change that.

Today, we work directly with smallholder farmers across Ethiopia’s most renowned coffee regions, sourcing traceable microlots and connecting them to roasters who care about quality and story.
